Transport

Since the wedding ceremony and reception are at two locations, you have the option to drive from Kausland Church to Panorama Hotel yourself after the ceremony.

Alternatively, if you need assistance with transportation, please let us know by phone.

Accommodation

The Kausland Church and Panorama Hotel are about an hour's drive from Bergen city center. For convenience in transportation between the church and hotel, we recommend staying at the Panorama Hotel.

The Panorama Hotel offers a 10% discount on accommodation from Friday to Sunday: 2890 NOK for a single room and 3390 NOK for a double room, both with breakfast, before the 10% discount.

Please contact us by phone as soon as possible to reserve rooms or for more information. We need to know how many guests want to stay at the hotel so that we can put in reservations for you, then you can pay on arrival at the hotel.
